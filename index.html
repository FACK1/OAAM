About

OAAM, Inc. owns and operates a Website that provides expert content for enabling users to answer questions, solve problems, learn something new, and find inspiration. Its platform enables users to search for expert content on various topics, such as home, travel, wine, health, music, food, jobs, money, style, technology, and others. About, Inc. was formerly known as About.com, Inc. and changed its name to About, Inc. in January 2002. The company was founded in 1996 and is based in New York, New York. As of September 24, 2012, About, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of IAC Search & Media, Inc.
